About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India and a great scientist. He played an important role in India's missile and nuclear programs. He was known as the "Missile Man of India".

Early Life

He was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Kalam studied aerospace engineering and worked with ISRO and DRDO.

Achievements

  • Developed India's missile technology
  • Played key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Received Bharat Ratna in 1997
  • Served as President of India (2002-2007)
